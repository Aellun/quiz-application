LearnHub is a dedicated platform aimed at facilitating employee learning, primarily through quizzes covering various workplace-relevant topics. Inspired by the absence of such a tool within developer organizations, LearnHub empowers HR departments to create quizzes aligned with their training initiatives, thereby fostering continuous learning among employees.

What to expect

Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence involves understanding and managing one's own emotions and those of others. It's a key skill for effective communication, teamwork, and leadership. Training in this area helps employees navigate complex interpersonal situations more effectively.


Conflict Resolution

This topic focuses on teaching employees how to handle disagreements and disputes in a constructive manner. It's crucial for maintaining a harmonious work environment and ensuring smooth operations.


Customer Service

With the increasing importance of customer satisfaction in today's business environment, training employees in customer service skills is essential.
